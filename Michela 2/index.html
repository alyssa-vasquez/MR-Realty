About Me

I have always loved real estate and architecture. My parents were born abroad, and I have been privileged to learn about different parts of the world. I was born in South Africa and fortunate enough to travel around the world. I began to appreciate all types of architecture in all the new places I visited. I grew up going to schools focused on arts and creativity and earned a degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Real Estate and Entrepreneurship. Surrounding myself with people who have similar passions really inspired me to continue my growth in design and Real Estate. Through experience and knowledge, I understand that beautiful architecture is not all it takes to make a house perfect - every family isn’t looking for the same thing. Being able to understand my client’s needs provide my clients with unparalled service gives me immense joy. Living in Los Angeles the past eight years, I have been fortunate enough to work alongside Los Angeles’ top agent Joe Babajian on various projects from Bel Air, Beverly Hills the Hollywood Hills as well as the premiere West Side of Los Angeles. I have assisted him on many deals and he has mentored me on every facet of real estate. I have experienced the different pockets of what LA’s Real Estate market and has to offer and I am not intimidated by any situation.
